Kid-Friendly Smoothies Even Picky Eaters Will Love

Tips for Kid-Approved Smoothies:

  • ✅ Use sweet fruits like banana and berries
  • ✅ Add a dollop of peanut butter or yogurt for creaminess
  • ✅ Get them involved—let them pick their colors and toppings
  • ✅ Freeze leftovers into popsicles!

Rainbow Smoothie

  • ½ banana
  • ½ cup strawberries
  • ½ cup mango
  • Small handful spinach (they won’t even taste it!)
  • ½ cup milk of choice
  • A squeeze of honey

Blend until smooth and serve with a fun straw!
