Product page

Preorder support NEW
Highlight Preorder products with automatic badges, button wording and messaging.
Product personalisation NEW
Allow your customers to personalise their purchases with our customisation support. Custom text and file uploads are added to your order and displayed on the order summary.
Supports product video & 3D renders
Create an immersive product experience by adding videos (supports MP4, YouTube, and Vimeo) as well as 3D image models so customers can feel more connected to your products.
Customizable swatches
Use automatic HEX color swatches or upload your own image to help customers quickly find the variant they’re looking for.
Cross-selling
Strategically recommend complementary or alternative products to increase upselling opportunities and AOV.
Stock level indicator
Generate a feeling of urgency with a stock indicator that highlights low inventory to customers, letting them know time’s running out to make a purchase!
Recently viewed & related products
Remind customers about previously viewed products and showcase related items from the same collection or unique items to increase the chances of making multiple sales!
Multiple block options NEW
Every store's product page requirements are unique, so we have an incredible 30 different block types to ensure you surface the right information for your customers to help increase conversion.
Linked products
Add individual products as variants on a separate product to showcase all your offerings on both product and collection pages.
Collapsible tabs
Pack more information into a smaller space using a clean and organized layout. Collapsible tabs allow you to break up key information like product descriptions, shipping prices, care instructions, ingredients and more.
Flexible, eye-catching layouts
Flexible product gallery styles and product form settings ensure you can put your customers’ focus where you want it - including options for large imagery and zoom!
