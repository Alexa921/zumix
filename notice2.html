Mango & Coconut Island Cooler

Flavour Base: Mango & Coconut

Category: Energy Boosting

Ingredients:

  • 1 Mango & Coconut smoothie
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup frozen pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Optional Garnish:

Pineapple wedge, toasted coconut, mint sprig

Instructions:

Blend the smoothie with orange juice, pineapple, and chia seeds for a tropical twist. Serve over crushed ice for a beachy, refreshing boost.
